Packages

Small Business Website (Custom Built)

A custom-built website, designed as you envision it. No e-commerce.

What does this mean?

Custom-built from scratch. We handle the technology — you just share your vision, brand or design ideas.

Why monthly maintenance?

Like a car, websites need regular checkups. We handle security, backups, updates, and fixes so you can focus on your business.

Best for

You want something unique and professional. You have a brand identity or specific vision. You prefer someone handling the website.

Development time: 2.5 weeks

One-time development fee: 6000 SEK

Monthly fee: 1,200 SEK/month of maintenance and updates

You cover: domain, hosting. You can provide content and images before or after the project starts.

Package — Build & Handover WordPress or Squarespace

A website built with WordPress (a simple content management system). E-commerce is possible.

What does this mean?

Built with CMS so you can update content yourself. It's like a word processor for websites.

Best for

You want control and independence. You're comfortable learning the admin interface.

Development time: 2 weeks

One-time development fee: 3,000 SEK (includes 1 training session)

You cover: CMS subscription and domain. You provide content and images before the project starts.
