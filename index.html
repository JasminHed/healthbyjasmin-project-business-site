Health by Jasmin

Health by Jasmin is run by Jasmin Hedlund, who has been practicing Ashtanga Yoga and Ayurveda for over thirteen years

She places great importance on practicing what she teaches. At Health by Jasmin you can find ashtanga classes, ayurveda treatments and much more.

With a calm and gentle approach, she strives to offer these practices in their most natural form and hopes to inspire others to self care and wellbeing.
