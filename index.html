About

Health by Jasmin is run by Jasmin Hedlund, who has been practicing Ashtanga Yoga and Ayurveda for over 15 years. She places great importance on embodying the practices she teaches.Jasmin offers Ashtanga Yoga classes, Ayurvedic health consultations, massage, courses, workshops, and more in Stockholm. She collaborates with fellow yoga practitioners and organizes annual retreats focusing on Ashtanga Yoga and Ayurveda.

Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga Yoga is a practice where the breath is at the core, synchronized with gentle, dynamic movements. By following a set sequence of postures, an inner rhythm is cultivated that promotes balance, presence, and transformation. The practice is an inward journey that strengthens both body and mind, with a focus on calmness and gradual progress at your own pace.

Ayurveda

Ayurveda offers us the knowledge and tools to strengthen and heal ourselves, both physically and mentally.

The word Ayurveda comes from Sanskrit and means “the knowledge of life.” Originating in India, it is one of the world’s oldest systems of health.

One of the most well-known aspects of Ayurveda is the dosha theory, which describes each individual’s unique personality and body type.

Retreats

For those seeking deeper recovery and inner balance, our retreat offers the perfect weekend with daily Ashtanga Yoga, nourishing food, and Ayurveda.

The retreat is held at a peaceful location near Stockholm, where you’ll have time to unwind, enjoy nature, and take part in yoga, sauna baths, and inspiring workshops, all designed to provide you with a complete and relaxing experience.
