About Health by Jasmin

Health by Jasmin is run by Jasmin Hedlund, who has been practicing Ashtanga Yoga and Ayurveda for over 15 years. She places great importance on embodying the practices she teaches. Jasmin offers Ashtanga Yoga classes, Ayurvedic health consultations, massage, courses, workshops, and more in Stockholm. She collaborates with fellow yoga practitioners and organizes annual retreats focusing on Ashtanga Yoga and Ayurveda.

Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga Yoga is a practice where the breath is at the core, synchronized with gentle, dynamic movements.

Ayurveda

Ayurveda offers us the knowledge and tools to strengthen and heal ourselves, both physically and mentally.

Workshops

The workshop on Yoga and Ayurveda offers a holistic approach to wellness, blending ancient practices of mindful movement, meditation, and natural healing to promote balance and vitality.

Retreats

For those seeking deeper recovery and inner balance, our retreat offers the perfect weekend with daily Ashtanga Yoga, nourishing food, and Ayurveda.
